About Us

Welcome to TasteMYStreets Project!

We are a passionate collective students from UTAR (Univsersity Tunku Abdul Rahman), united by a shared love for the vibrant, aromatic, and profoundly delicious world of Malaysian cuisine and culture.

Inspired by the variety of local cuisines that existed in Kampar that remain unexplored, We intend to unwrap the beauty of local cuisine via our website.

Our mission is simple: to discover the hidden gems of Malaysian food that exist within the country itself.

Join us in our journey of exploring the delicious cuisine that is hidden in plain sight!
